Welcome to AfyaRekod

Afyarekod is an AI-driven two-way health data management platform for storage and communication used by patients, doctors, health workers, and hospitals.

Store Health

Patients can keep relevant health history including medical records, prescriptions and doctors notes in a secure, easy to access data plaform.

Data Driven Solution for Decision

Health organizations can collect patient-authorized data and gain insight on how to improve their health through AI data modelling.

Patient Education

Health practitioners and organizations can push their health content to patients in form of surveys, health articles and insights on medications.

About Us

We are a Consumer-Driven Health Data Platform Built on AI and Blockchain Technology.

We have a strong consumer focus and a full product suite built for medical facilities and organizations of all sizes to manage their inpatients and outpatients.

As an AI-driven two-way health data management platform, our goal is to provide you with storage and communication. We serve various groups of people including patients, doctors, health practitioners and organizations.

Our goal is to help patients manage their health records and stay on top of their health. We provide medical facilities, governments, and other partners a data-driven platform to store health data and access patient information anytime and anywhere.

We have built a robust platform that also provides a number of other tools and features for medical facilities and health workers to manage patients in real-time.
