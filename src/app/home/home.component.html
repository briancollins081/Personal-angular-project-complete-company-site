General Services

We got you covered in the following key areas.

Data Storage

Store your relevant data as a patient and access it later, or share it with your doctor(s)/health organization(s).

Great Analytics

As a health organization, get a platform that gives insight and trends in your accessible/authorized patient data.

Realtime Chat

Organizations, doctors and patients can communicate with each other through the live chat support.

Patient Education

Patients can access articles and health content published by their health organizations or their doctors.

Telehealth

Doctors are given a way to reach their patients through online appointments, chats and information sharing.

Income Generation

Patients can generate income by participating in organization/institution based surveys.

Self Assessments

Take a self assessment of your health both in the mobile app and in the USSD application.

Daily Health Updates

Update your daily health diary to keep track of your symptoms, diseases, medications and any prescriptions you take.
